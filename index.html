Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What courses do you offer?

A

We offer a variety of tech courses including Frontend Development, Backend Development, and Database Administration. Each course is designed to provide practical, industry-relevant skills.

Q

How long are the courses?

A

Most of our courses run for 12 weeks. We offer both full-time and part-time options to accommodate different schedules.

Q

Are there any prerequisites?

A

While specific prerequisites vary by course, most require basic computer literacy and English proficiency. Some advanced courses may require prior programming experience.

Q

What is the cost of the courses?

A

Course fees range from UGX 350K to UGX 500K. We offer flexible payment plans and scholarship opportunities for eligible students.

Q

Do you offer job placement assistance?

A

Yes, we provide career support including resume building, interview preparation, and connections to our network of hiring partners.
